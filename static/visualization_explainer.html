Ranked Placement Atlas

The visualization

Two linked views: a cross-%%bin_term%% heatmap that collapses a date window into one aggregated snapshot, and a per-%%bin_term%% time series that unfolds that snapshot back across individual dates.

%%Item_term%%s used below APX BRT CFD DLT ETR

Row = one %%bin_term%% A %%bin_term%% is the top-level grouping unit. Each %%bin_term%% occupies one horizontal band, labeled by its ID.
Column = one position Position 1 is the leftmost column (highest-ranked slot). Columns increase to the right up to the position-range limit.
Cell color = dominant %%item_term%% The color shows which %%item_term%% held that slot most often across the selected date window. Hover any cell for the exact label, rank, segment, and winning share.
Rank in the hover tooltip Each %%bin_term%%'s global rank appears in the hover tooltip, not in the row label. It drives the %%Bin_term%% Rank sort and the rank-range filter.

Hovering a cell reveals its full detail:

Basin  ·  Rank 12  ·  NA
Position 3  ·  Item BRT
Date-win share: 83%

Date-win share = fraction of dates in the selected window on which BRT was the plurality winner at this position for this bin. 83% means 5 of 6 snapshots.
